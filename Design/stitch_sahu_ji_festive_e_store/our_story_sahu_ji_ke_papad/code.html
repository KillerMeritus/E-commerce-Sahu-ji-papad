The Sahu Family

साहू परिवार की कहानी (Since 2005)

It started in a small kitchen in Rajasthan, where the scent of roasted cumin and sun-dried lentils was a daily comfort. Our grandmother, with hands weathered by love and labor, crafted papads not just as a side dish, but as a centerpiece of family gatherings.

Since 2005, we have carried forward this humble tradition. We don't use factories or artificial drying rooms. Every batch of Sahu Ji Ke Papad is rolled by hand and dried under the golden Indian sun, preserving the authentic crunch and earthy flavors that define a true homemade meal.
