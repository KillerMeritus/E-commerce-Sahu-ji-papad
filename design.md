# Design System Strategy: Saffron Hearth (Sahu Ji Ke Papad)

## 1. Overview & Creative North Star
The **"Saffron Hearth"** design system is built to evoke the feeling of a trusted neighbor's homemade brand that has successfully moved online. It rejects the "luxury/premium" dark-mode aesthetic in favor of a bright, airy, and honest "apna brand" feel. It is clean, organized, and professional without being corporate.

**Core Principles:**
- **Familiarity (Apnapan):** Using natural bilingual copy (Hindi + English) and a warm color palette.
- **Honesty (Imandari):** Clean photography, simple thin borders, and plenty of whitespace.
- **Quality at Scale:** "Saste mein accha" — high-quality homemade food at affordable prices.
- **Trust (Vishwas):** Prominent trust badges and a focus on family heritage.

---

## 2. Visual Language

### 2.1 Color Palette
The palette is inspired by Indian kitchens and spices, specifically saffron and turmeric, set against a clean, breathable background.
- **Primary (Saffron):** `#F97316` - Used for primary CTAs, prices, and active states. Approachable and appetite-stimulating.
- **Secondary (Turmeric):** `#FBBF24` - Used for badges, special offers, and highlights. Warm and festive.
- **Background (Cream):** `#FAFAF7` - An off-white base that feels softer and more natural than pure white.
- **Text Primary:** `#1C1917` - Near-black for high legibility.
- **Text Secondary:** `#78716C` - Warm grey for Hindi translations and secondary info.
- **Accents:** Indian Green (`#16A34A`) for "Veg" indicators and "Homemade" badges.

### 2.2 Typography
A bilingual system that balances heritage with modern readability.
- **Headlines:** *Tiro Devanagari Hindi* — Provides an authentic Indian feel that is grounded and readable.
- **Subheadings/UI:** *Poppins SemiBold* — Clean and friendly.
- **Body:** *Poppins Regular* — For effortless reading on desktop and mobile.
- **Hindi Secondary:** *Noto Sans Devanagari* — Used for sub-labels and supporting text.

### 2.3 UI Elements
- **Borders:** Thin 1px borders (`#E7E5E4`) are preferred over heavy shadows.
- **Corners:** 8px for cards, 6px for buttons, and 20px for pills to maintain a soft but organized look.
- **Dividers:** Simple, clean saffron-tinted lines or thin grey lines.
- **Imagery:** Natural light, wooden/cloth surfaces, home kitchen feel. Avoid "studio white" backgrounds.

---

## 3. Key Components

### 3.1 Product Cards
- **Structure:** Square image top, text details bottom.
- **Badges:** Green "Homemade" leaf icon top-left; Turmeric "SAVE X%" top-right.
- **Price Display:** The ₹ symbol is prominent, using Saffron Bold text.
- **CTA:** Full-width Saffron "Add to Cart" button.

### 3.2 Navigation & Footer
- **Navbar:** Clean white background with a thin saffron bottom border. Logo features a simple papad icon.
- **Footer:** Off-white background with a prominent WhatsApp contact feature and "Made with ❤️ in India" credit.

### 3.3 Trust Section
- Uses a 4-badge horizontal row with simple saffron icons and grey text to quickly communicate "Homemade," "No Preservatives," "State-wide Delivery," and "Family Recipe."

---

## 4. Tone of Voice
- **Bilingual:** Mixes English and Hindi naturally (e.g., "Sabse Zyada Bikne Wale / Our Bestsellers").
- **Warm & Direct:** Avoids marketing fluff. Sounds like a conversation with a family member who is proud of their cooking.
